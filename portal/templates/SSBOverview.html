Super Signal Boy

Introduction

Super Signal Boy is a mobile game currently in development. The goal is to guide Signal Boy to each levels exit by binding control of puzzle elements to 3 available on screen buttons.

It's very early days on this project but its a great learning opportunity for me when it comes to mobile development and more complex game mechanics. I'm levaraging a lot of what I learned in developing FLO in developing this game with best practices in mind.
